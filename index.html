About Us

Our group was started in October 2019 as a community built on two principles; a welcoming environment and car diversity. As a result, over the last 12 months the group has grown from a small group of local Boerne residents, to dozens of locals from all over the South Texas hill country bringing some diverse and unique builds to our events, leading to our creed:

Anything is welcome.

As we look to the future, we hope to incorporate more local businesses and more members in the community as we widen our event catalog and our positive impact on the community at large...
